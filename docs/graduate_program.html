Graduate program

BIHE offers a unique graduate-level educational experience incorporating both face-to-face and online educational opportunities. BIHE offers 15 academic and professional master’s degree programs. Each program is based on international standards; courses are taught by accomplished faculty members and professionals with distinguished academic achievements.

Students can apply to the program after completing a vigorous four-year undergraduate program at BIHE. They should demonstrate both academic strengths and research abilities to be considered for the program of their interests. The master’s programs range from two to three years.

Students can apply to the program after completing a vigorous four-year undergraduate program at BIHE. They should demonstrate both academic strengths and research abilities to be considered for the program of their interests. The master’s programs range from two to three years. Master’s programs at BIHE are founded on the following academic goals:

  • Mastery of subject areas
  • Theoretical and applied knowledge of research methods
  • Critical insights and practical skills for employment
  • Ability to conduct independent research
  • Academic competence for success in doctoral level studies and scholarly pursuits

The 15 master’s programs currently offered at BIHE are:

  • Master in Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Construction Management
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Counseling Psychology
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Curriculum Development
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Educational Psychology
  • Master in General Psychology
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Hydraulic Structures
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in International Law
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Neurosciences
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Persian Literature
  • Master of Public Health (M.P.H)
  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Sociology
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Software Engineering
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Structural Engineering
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Transportation Engineering
